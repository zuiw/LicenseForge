# Reciprocal Patent License, Version 1.0 (RPL-1.0)

**Copyright (C) 2026 [Your Name or Organization]**

A patent-focused copyleft license that grants broad patent rights to all users
of the software, but terminates those rights for any party that initiates
patent litigation related to the software.

## Preamble

The RPL is designed for projects in patent-dense fields where the primary
risk is patent litigation rather than source-code hoarding.  It provides
standard permissive terms for copyright while adding mutual patent protection:
anyone who uses the software receives a license to all relevant patents held
by the contributors, and anyone who attacks the project with patents loses
their own rights.
