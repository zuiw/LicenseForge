## Preamble

The SCCL addresses supply chain transparency. If the Licensed Work is used in any product or service, all components in that product's supply chain—including direct and transitive dependencies, build tools, CI/CD infrastructure, and deployment tooling—must be open source under a copyleft or compatible license. This prevents the common pattern of using open source components within an otherwise proprietary supply chain.
