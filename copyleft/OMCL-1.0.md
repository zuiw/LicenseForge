## Preamble

The OMCL ensures complete transparency of all changes. Unlike standard copyleft licenses that only require source disclosure upon distribution, OMCL requires that all modifications—even those used internally and never distributed—must be published publicly in real time. The rationale is that all improvements should benefit the entire community immediately, not be hoarded privately.
