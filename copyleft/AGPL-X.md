(SAGPL) family, adding stronger disclosure requirements while preserving

## Preamble

AGPL-X extends the AGPLv3 with shorter cure periods, mandatory upstream
contribution, broader source disclosure requirements (including build
environments), and stronger network transparency provisions.  It is designed
for projects that want significantly stronger protection than AGPLv3 but do
not require the extreme measures of SAGPL.
