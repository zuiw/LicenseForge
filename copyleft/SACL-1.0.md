## Preamble

The SACL recognizes that software patent laws vary by jurisdiction. Copyleft obligations apply only in jurisdictions where software is not patentable or where broad patent exceptions exist. In jurisdictions with strong software patent protection, the license converts to permissive terms to avoid creating additional patent risk for users.
