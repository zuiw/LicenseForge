## Preamble

The TIMEL provides strong copyleft protection until a specified future date, at which point all copyleft obligations expire and the license converts to MIT terms. Unlike SUNL-1.0 (which uses a fixed 5-year term from release), TIMEL uses an absolute date set by the licensor, allowing precise control over when the copyleft period ends.
