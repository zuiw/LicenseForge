A copyleft license that extends requirements transitively: derivative works of derivative works must also remain under the same license.

## Preamble

The CHCL ensures that copyleft requirements propagate through the entire chain of derivative works. While standard copyleft licenses require direct derivatives to remain open, CHCL explicitly requires that any work derived from a derivative (and so on ad infinitum) must also remain under CHCL. This eliminates the "two-hop loophole" where a derivative is released openly, but a further derivative from that derivative is closed.
