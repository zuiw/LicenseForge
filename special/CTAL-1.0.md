## Preamble

The CTAL governs software used in managing clinical trials. It ensures that clinical trial software meets regulatory standards for patient safety and data reliability.

"Informed Consent" means a patient's documented agreement to participate after understanding risks and benefits.

"Adverse Event" means any untoward medical occurrence in a trial participant.

"GCP" means Good Clinical Practice standards (ICH E6).

### 2. Informed Consent Tracking.

The Software must ensure:
- **a)** 21 CFR Part 11 compliance (electronic signatures, audit trail);

The Software must:
- **a)** Provide structured Adverse Event forms;
- **b)** Automatically notify relevant parties within 24 hours;
