## Preamble

The LRL is designed for projects that want to encourage contributions while
minimizing compliance burden for small changes.  Small modifications (less
than 15% of the codebase changed) can be kept private.  Larger modifications
trigger full copyleft disclosure.  This creates a "safe harbor" for minor
fixes while ensuring that substantial derivative works are shared.
