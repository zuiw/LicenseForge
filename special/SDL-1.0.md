## Preamble

The SDL addresses the unique nature of synthetic data: data generated by AI
models rather than collected from real-world sources.  It permits free use
and distribution of synthetic data for training, testing, and research,
while requiring that the method of generation be documented and that
synthetic data be clearly labeled as such to prevent confusion with
real-world data.
