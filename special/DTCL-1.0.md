## Preamble

The DTCL governs software for data trusts and cooperatives where data is managed on behalf of a group. It requires fiduciary duties to data beneficiaries (individuals whose data is held), affirmative consent for data use, and proportional distribution of benefits derived from the data. It implements data stewardship principles.

## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### 0. Definitions.

"This License" refers to version 1.0 of the Data Trust License (DTCL-1.0).

"The Software" means the data trust or cooperative management system licensed under this License.

"Beneficiary" means the individual whose data is held in the trust.

"Trust Purpose" means the stated purpose for which the trust collects and uses data.

"Benefit Distribution" means the allocation of value derived from trust data.

### 1. Permissions.

You may use, copy, modify, and distribute the Software, subject to the data trust conditions below.

### 2. Fiduciary Duties.

Operators owe Beneficiaries duties of:
- **a)** Loyalty: act in Beneficiaries' best interests;
- **b)** Care: protect data with reasonable security;
- **c)** Transparency: disclose all data usage.

### 3. Beneficiary Consent.

Data collection and use requires:
- **a)** Affirmative opt-in consent;
- **b)** Granular consent per data category;
- **c)** Right to withdraw consent at any time;
- **d)** Consent revocation without penalty.

### 4. Benefit Distribution.

If the trust generates value from data:
- **a)** Benefits must be distributed proportionally to Beneficiaries;
- **b)** Distribution methodology must be transparent;
- **c)** Administrative costs must be capped at a disclosed percentage.

### 5. Governance.

Beneficiaries must have voting rights on material changes to Trust Purpose or benefit structure.

### 6. Termination.

Operating without Beneficiary consent or misusing data outside Trust Purpose terminates rights. No cure for misuse.
