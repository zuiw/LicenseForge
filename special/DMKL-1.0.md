A license for data marketplaces requiring transparent pricing, data quality grading, provenance verification, and escrow for data delivery.

## Preamble

The DMKL governs data marketplace platforms where data is bought and sold. It requires transparent pricing with no hidden fees, data quality grading so buyers can assess fitness, provenance verification to confirm data authenticity, and escrow mechanisms ensuring data delivery upon payment.

## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### 0. Definitions.

"This License" refers to version 1.0 of the Data Marketplace License (DMKL-1.0).

"The Software" means the data marketplace platform licensed under this License.

"Data Listing" means a dataset or data product offered for sale on the marketplace.

"Data Grade" means a quality rating assigned to a Data Listing.

### 1. Permissions.

You may use, copy, modify, and distribute the Software, subject to the marketplace conditions below.

### 2. Price Transparency.

The Software must display all costs associated with a Data Listing upfront, including any platform fees, royalty obligations, and usage-based charges. No undisclosed fees may be added at checkout.

### 3. Data Quality Grading.

Each Data Listing must have a Data Grade indicating:
- **a)** Completeness (percentage of expected records);
- **b)** Accuracy (estimated error rate);
- **c)** Freshness (last update timestamp);
- **d)** Coverage (temporal and geographic scope).

### 4. Provenance Verification.

The Software must record and display the provenance chain for each Data Listing: original source, collection methodology, and all intermediate handlers.

### 5. Delivery Escrow.

The Software must implement payment escrow: buyer funds are held until data is delivered and verified, with dispute resolution for delivery failures.

### 6. Rating System.

Buyers and sellers may rate each other, with ratings displayed publicly. The Software must prevent rating manipulation.

### 7. Termination.

Operating without price transparency or provenance records terminates rights. A 60-day cure period applies.
