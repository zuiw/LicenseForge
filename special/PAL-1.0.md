A permissive license focused on patent transparency, requiring users to
disclose known patents that may read on the software.

## Preamble

The PAL addresses a specific problem: users of open-source software sometimes
hold patents that read on the code, but do not disclose them, creating
legal risk for the entire community.  PAL requires that anyone who
distributes the software and knows of relevant patents must disclose that
knowledge.  It is not a copyleft license for copyright purposes—code can
be used permissively—but it creates a patent disclosure obligation.
