international sanctions, export controls, or legal restrictions on use in
specific jurisdictions.  It allows the copyright holder to designate
permitted and prohibited geographic regions.  Use in prohibited regions is
not authorized.  The goal is to provide a legally clear mechanism for
geographic restrictions without requiring custom licenses per project.
