# Time-Activated License, Version 1.0 (TAL-1.0)

**Copyright (C) 2026 [Your Name or Organization]**

A time-limited copyleft license that automatically converts to a permissive
license after a specified period, enabling dual-phase licensing for academic
and commercial projects.

## Preamble

The TAL gives projects the best of both worlds: strong copyleft protection
during the initial "protection period" (typically 2-4 years), followed by
automatic conversion to a permissive license.  This encourages early adoption
with disclosure obligations while ensuring long-term permissive availability
of the code.  It is ideal for academic projects that want to control their
IP during development while eventually joining the commons.
