## Preamble

The FDL addresses the ethical challenges of sharing data that contains
information about people.  It permits use of the data for research and
analysis, but explicitly prohibits attempts to re-identify individuals,
discriminate against protected groups, or use the data in ways that harm
the data subjects.

- Re-identification of Data Subjects;
