A license for media content distribution platforms requiring content identification, rights management, and fair compensation to creators.

## Preamble

The MCDL governs platforms that distribute digital media content (video, audio, images). It requires content identification to prevent infringement, transparent rights management, and fair compensation mechanisms for content creators. It balances platform operations with creator rights.

## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### 0. Definitions.

"This License" refers to version 1.0 of the Media Content Distribution License (MCDL-1.0).

"The Software" means the content distribution platform licensed under this License.

"Content" means video, audio, image, or text files distributed through the platform.

"Content Creator" means the person or entity that created the Content.

### 1. Permissions.

You may use, copy, modify, and distribute the Software, subject to the media distribution conditions below.

### 2. Content Identification.

The Software must support:
- **a)** Content fingerprinting for identification;
- **b)** Rights holder designation per Content item;
- **c)** Usage tracking and reporting.

### 3. Rights Management.

You must:
- **a)** Respect Content Creator licensing choices;
- **b)** Provide clear copyright attribution;
- **c)** Support takedown requests for infringing Content;
- **d)** Not remove or alter digital rights metadata.

### 4. Fair Compensation.

If the Software monetizes Content, you must:
- **a)** Disclose revenue sharing terms to Content Creators;
- **b)** Pay Content Creators their agreed share within 60 days;
- **c)** Provide transparent earnings reports.

### 5. Termination.

Distributing Content without identification or fair compensation terminates rights. A 60-day cure period applies.
