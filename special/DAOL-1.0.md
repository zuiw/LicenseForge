### 2. Governance Fairness.

If you deploy the Software for a DAO, you must ensure:
- **a)** Voting power is transparent and auditable;
- **b)** Proposal submission is accessible to all qualified members;
- **c)** Vote outcomes are verifiable on-chain or through cryptographic proofs;
- **d)** A minimum voting period prevents rushed decisions.

### 3. Treasury Transparency.

All Treasury transactions must be:
- **a)** Recorded on a public ledger;
- **b)** Subject to member approval for above-threshold amounts;
- **c)** Auditable by any member on demand.

### 4. Governance Attack Protection.

The Software must implement:
- **a)** Mitigation against flash loan attacks on voting;
- **b)** Timelocks on critical parameter changes;
- **c)** Emergency pause mechanism for detected attacks;
- **d)** Gradual vote power accumulation (anti-whale).

### 5. Fork and Exit Rights.

Members must have the right to:
- **a)** Fork the DAO with their share of assets;
- **b)** Exit with their proportional contribution;
- **c)** Access the member list and governance history.
