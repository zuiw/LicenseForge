### 3. Content Moderation.

You must maintain and enforce clear content moderation policies that
include:
- **a)** Published terms and conditions with content rules;
- **b)** A mechanism for Users to report illegal content;
- **c)** A statement of reasons for any content removal or restriction;
- **d)** An internal complaint handling system;
- **e)** Designation of a single point of contact for authorities.

### 4. User Redress.

You must provide Users with:
- **a)** A clear explanation of moderation decisions;
- **b)** The ability to appeal moderation decisions;
- **c)** Access to out-of-court dispute settlement.

### 5. Risk Assessment.

If you operate a very large platform (over 45 million users in the EU),
you must conduct annual risk assessments covering:
- Illegal content dissemination;
- Negative effects on fundamental rights;
- Manipulation of the Platform through disinformation.
