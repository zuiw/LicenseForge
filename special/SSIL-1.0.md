## Preamble

The SSIL governs software for self-sovereign identity platforms, decentralized identifiers (DIDs), and verifiable credential (VC) systems. It requires that users retain full control over their identity data, that identifiers are portable across providers, and that credentials are cryptographically verifiable without reliance on central authorities.

## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### 0. Definitions.

"Self-Sovereign Identity" means a digital identity system where the user exclusively controls their identity data.

"DID" means a Decentralized Identifier resolvable via a distributed ledger or similar system.

"Verifiable Credential" means a cryptographically signed attestation.

### 2. User Control.
