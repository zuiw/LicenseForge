A license that grants an explicit patent peace covenant and prohibits patent
litigation against users of the software.

## Preamble

The APL addresses the threat of software patents to open-source development.
It requires that all contributors grant a patent license to users, and that
anyone who uses the Software cannot assert software patents against other
users.  Patent litigation automatically terminates all rights.
